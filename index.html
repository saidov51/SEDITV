Is it possible to test your service before purchase ? icon

Yes, absolutely. You just need to request a code via the button below to obtain, within 48 hours, a code allowing you to access our services as well as information on its implementation.

Can I cancel my subscription ? icon

Unfortunately, the codes are assigned to only one person and we cannot reassign them afterwards. You will therefore have to wait until the end of the subscription.

What do I need ? icon

You will need either a device running Android (smartphone, tablet, Android box, mini PC, etc.) to enjoy our application, or have a system on which to install Kodi (Windows, MAC, Linux, etc.)

Are your services stable ? icon

We do everything we can to ensure quality service, especially during peak periods. Please note that our servers are now able to handle the current load without any problems.

How to pay ? icon

We accept payments by MasterCard, Visa, Visa Debit and American Express credit cards. Everything is done in an ultra secure way via our service provider. All these payments can be made via PAYPAL.

How long will it take to receive my access ? icon

Generally, your accesses are sent to you, by email, in less than 12 hours. We also tell you the steps to follow to be able to quickly enjoy your IPTV subscription.

Is the subscription freeze-free ? icon

Our servers are checked every day and we guarantee good quality sharing without freezes.
